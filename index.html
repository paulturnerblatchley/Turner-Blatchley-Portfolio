Videography

My video production approach combines strategic planning, creative direction, and technical execution. I develop compelling visual stories that convey key messages in an engaging format, working through the entire production process from concept and scripting to filming and post-production.

Superintendent's Newsletter Video

Created monthly video updates featuring the Superintendent highlighting key initiatives, visiting school sites, and addressing important educational topics for the county's education community.

Impact:

The video newsletter series increased staff engagement by 47% compared to text-only newsletters. Videos averaged 3,500+ views per month across platforms, with an average watch time of 4:32 minutes.

Golden Bell Award Program

Produced a series of videos highlighting innovative educational programs that received the prestigious Golden Bell Award, showcasing their impact on student achievement and school improvement.

Impact:

The Golden Bell video series was incorporated into the statewide awards ceremony and used by winning districts in their community outreach. The videos contributed to a 28% increase in award applications the following year.
